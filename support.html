Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add someone?
    Tap the + button on the home screen, select a contact, choose how often you'd like to be reminded, and tap "Start Reminders."
  • How do I change the reminder time?
    Tap the gear icon on the home screen to open Settings. Use the time picker to set your preferred reminder time, and optionally apply it to all existing contacts.
  • Why does Undrift need access to my contacts?
    Undrift reads your contact names and phone numbers so you can choose who to track. Your contact data never leaves your device.
  • Can I change how often I'm reminded about someone?
    Yes. Tap on any contact card on the home screen to edit their reminder frequency, call type, or next reminder date.
  • How do I remove someone?
    Swipe left on their card on the home screen and tap Delete.
  • Does Undrift work without an internet connection?
    Yes. Everything runs locally on your device. No internet required.
